My Story

Decades of Design Expertise

For over 35 years, I've had the privilege of working as an independent designer on a wide range of residential and commercial projects. From complete interior and exterior projects as well as custom kitchens, baths, custom built-ins and one-of-a-kind furniture pieces, I've honed my skills across the design spectrum.

Collaborating with Design Professionals

For over 35 years, I've had the privilege of working as an independent designer on a wide range of residential and commercial projects. My design expertise spans space planning, 3D presentations, and detailed construction documentation.
